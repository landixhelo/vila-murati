About Us

The best vacation destination!

Situated in Ksamil, within 300 metres of Ksamil Beach and 500 metres of Bora Bora Beach, Vila Murati features accommodation with a garden as well as free private parking for guests who drive. The property offers soundproof units and is set 600 metres from Ksamil Beach 7.

The guest house will provide guests with air-conditioned units offering a wardrobe, a kettle, a safety deposit box, a flat-screen TV, a balcony and a private bathroom with a bidet. Free WiFi is accessible to all guests, while certain rooms come with a terrace.
